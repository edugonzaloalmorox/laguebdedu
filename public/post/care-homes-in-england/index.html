Plotting with leaflet

 Posted on November 9, 2018

Certainly aging is a policy concern is a developed economy.

Load data

The Care Quality Commmission releases information on care homes on a monthly basis. These are quite complete and provide several pieces of useful information. For example, the quality that a care home has after being inspected.

   clean_care = care_homes %>% 
  select(location_id, location_name, location_latest_overall_rating, location_longitude, location_latitude)

How is the quality of care homes

The quality of care homes is inspected regularly. Care homes can obtain 4 potential ratings_ Outstanding, Good, Requires improvement and Inadequate

   clean_care %>%
    filter(!is.na(location_latest_overall_rating)) %>%
    mutate(quality_rating = fct_relevel(location_latest_overall_rating, "Outstanding", "Good", "Requires Improvement", "Inadequate")) %>%
    ggplot(., aes(quality_rating)) +
  geom_histogram(stat = "count")  +
  labs(title = "Quality ratings", x = "Rating", y = "")

Plot the map

With leaflet when can draw an interactive map and locate the care homes

clean_care %>% 
    leaflet()  %>% 
       addProviderTiles("CartoDB") %>% 
     addCircleMarkers(
  lng = ~location_longitude, lat = ~location_latitude, radius = 0.005, 
  popup = ~location_name, color = "Blue")
